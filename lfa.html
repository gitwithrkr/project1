About Our Academy

Training Grounds Image

Developing Future Stars

Legend's Football Academy at Taranjubari is dedicated to nurturing young talent and developing well-rounded athletes. Our professional coaching staff focuses on technical skills, tactical understanding, physical development, and mental strength.

We believe in creating an environment where players can thrive, learn, and enjoy the beautiful game while developing discipline, teamwork, and leadership skills that will benefit them throughout life.

We are proud to announce our collaboration with Mayoor School to promote football and holistic development in young athletes. This partnership helps us offer school-integrated training programs and wider opportunities for our students.
